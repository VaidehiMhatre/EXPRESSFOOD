FAQs

How do I place an order with your tiffin delivery service?
Ordering with us is simple! Visit our website or mobile app, select your preferred tiffin options, choose your delivery slot, add any desired extras or dietary preferences, and proceed to payment.
What are the available delivery slots?
We offer flexible delivery slots throughout the day to cater to your schedule. Our delivery slots typically cover breakfast, lunch, and dinner times, with options for early morning and late-night deliveries.
Can I customize my tiffin order based on dietary preferences?
Absolutely! We understand that everyone has unique dietary needs. You can easily customize your tiffin order to include vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, or other dietary preferences. Simply select your options during the ordering process.We also offer a variety of add-ons to enhance your dining experience.
What are the subscription options available for your tiffin delivery service?
We offer flexible subscription plans to suit your needs. You can choose from weekly, monthly, quarterly (3 months), semi-annual (6 months), and annual subscription options.Subscribing to our tiffin delivery plan offers several benefits, including discounted pricing, priority access to delivery slots, hassle-free ordering with automated renewals, and the convenience of having nutritious meals delivered to your doorstep regularly.
Can I cancel my tiffin subscription and request a refund if needed
Yes, you can cancel your tiffin subscription at any time, and we offer a flexible refund policy. If you decide to cancel your subscription before the next billing cycle, you'll receive a prorated refund for the remaining unused portion of your subscription period. Simply contact our customer support team or manage your subscription online to initiate the cancellation and refund process. Please note that refunds may take a few business days to reflect in your account, depending on your payment method and bank processing times.
